1  Introduction

The Routing System II program was developed at the Laboratory of Hydraulic Constructions (LCH) at the Ecole Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL) (Dubois and Boillat 2000; García Hernández et al. 2007).

The program presented hereafter, RS MINERVE, is based on the same concept than Routing System II. RS MINERVE is developed by the CREALP and HydroCosmos SA with the collaboration of the Laboratory of Hydraulic Constructions (LCH) at the Ecole Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL) and the Universitat Politècnica de València (UPV).
